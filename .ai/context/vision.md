# Product Vision

Source of truth: `docs/PRODUCT_BIBLE.md`

## Purpose

NextBite is a premium consumer mobile app for real-world food exploration.

The product helps users decide what to eat by making nearby restaurants feel discoverable, collectible, and motivating.

## Core Principle

**Exploration > Search.**

Users should discover food through exploration — not endless scrolling, filtering, or decision paralysis.

## Target Experience

- Open the app → See a map of nearby restaurants
- Explore visually → Markers, cards, and motion guide discovery
- Decide confidently → Calories, health score, walking distance
- Save intentionally → Collections, not bookmarks
- Share playfully → Challenges, streaks, reactions

## Brand

- Premium but warm
- Food-first, interface-second
- Health as helpful information, not clinical tracking
- Timeless over trendy

## Non-Vision

NextBite is NOT:

- A delivery app
- A calorie tracker
- A social media feed
- An enterprise analytics platform
- A marketplace
