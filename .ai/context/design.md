# Design Context

Source of truth: `docs/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md`

## Design Principles

### Usability over decoration
Every element serves the user's goal. Remove anything that doesn't.

### Timeless over trendy
Avoid design fads. The app should feel premium five years from now.

### Exploration before search
Discovery first. Search is a fallback, not the primary mode.

### Food before interface
Photography and food content take priority over UI chrome.

### One primary purpose per screen
A screen does one thing well. No Swiss-army-knife interfaces.

### Health as helpful, not clinical
Information about calories, protein, and walking distance should feel empowering, not judgmental.

### Motion that clarifies
Animation should communicate state changes or add restrained delight. Never decorative motion.

## Visual Direction

- Clean, minimal, premium
- Food-forward photography
- Warm, not cold
- Maps are the canvas — UI overlays are light
